Every Child Needs a Champion


CONSIDER BECOMING A MONTHLY GIVER


Make a difference in the lives of orphaned and vulnerable children year-round when you become a member of The OPA Village - our worldwide community of monthly donors.


You can help provide children in orphanages with food, education, health, caregivers, emotional support, and most importantly - a happier and healthier childhood. We can’t change the circumstances these children came from, but we can change their futures. A gift of just $20 a month can send a child to school for a year and ensure them a more promising future.

Gratitude Unleashed: Honoring Those Who Illuminate Hope


To those who have not yet donated but are considering it, I extend my sincere appreciation for your willingness to make a difference.
Your willingness to support the Orphan Association, whether through donations or other means, is deeply valued. Your compassion and empathy for those in need are truly admirable, and I thank you for considering how you can contribute to creating positive change in the lives of vulnerable children. Every act of kindness, no matter how small, has the power to make a meaningful impact. Thank you for your thoughtful consideration and for being a beacon of hope in our community.
